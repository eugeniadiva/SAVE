About us

S.A.V.E (Students for Animal Voices and Ethics) is a student-led animal group based in Sinarmas World Academy established in 2011. We have a goal which is to stand for and educate people on issues concerning animal rights and the environment within our local community.
As a team, we serve, raise awareness, and fundraise. For the next one and a half academic year, we will continue to support Shelter Pak Johan (SPJ) by donating money, dog food, and other goods. Shelter Pak Johan itself is a non-profit organization that specializes in helping animals in need.
