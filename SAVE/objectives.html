What do we aim to do?

S.A.V.E Objectives
  • Spread and raise the awareness of animal rights and endangered animals through social media (not only within the school community)
  • Collaborate with the shelter to get dogs adopted
  • Having a successful annual walkathon
  • Organize and carry out successful fundraisers to earn enough funds to help the expansion of the animal shelter
Learning Outcomes
  1. Demonstrate the skills and recognize the benefits of working collaboratively
  2. Demonstrate engagement with issues of global significance
  3. Recognize and consider the ethics of choices and actions
